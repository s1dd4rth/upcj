  - "Once the enhancement is approved, the new sanctioned amount replaces the original. Keep a copy of the updated Approval Letter."

Enhancement approvals can take up to 4 hours, and discharge cannot be settled until this is resolved. Alert the insurance desk at least a day before you expect to be discharged if costs are tracking above the original approval.
