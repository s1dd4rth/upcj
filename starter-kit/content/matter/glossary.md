---
terms:
  - term: "TPA"
    definition: "Third-Party Administrator. The intermediary between hospital and insurer that authorises cashless treatment."
  - term: "ICD-10"
    definition: "International Classification of Diseases, 10th revision. The diagnosis code the insurer's system requires — see the ICD-10 quick reference (back-matter)."
  - term: "UHID"
    definition: "Unique Hospital ID. The hospital's identifier for you — appears on every document they generate."
  - term: "Pre-authorisation"
    definition: "The TPA's approval to treat you on a cashless basis before admission. Distinct from the final claim settlement."
  - term: "Sum insured"
    definition: "The maximum the policy will pay in the policy year across all claims."
---
