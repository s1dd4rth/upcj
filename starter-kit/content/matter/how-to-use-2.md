---
title: "What each page looks like"
lede: "Three page types, each with a fixed layout you can recognise at a glance."
---

**Guide pages** — one per step. A black masthead names the step, a grey band shows the phase and path. "Say this" blocks (blue left-border, italic script) are verbatim what to tell the counter. "Check the document has" is the list of fields to verify before you walk away.

**Template pages** — one per document. Yellow operational note at the top names who fills it. A stamp box (45 mm square) sits bottom-right; a signature rule sits bottom-left. Hand these to the doctor or clerk, then keep the completed page.

**Front and back matter** — cover (page 1), these two sheets (pages 2–3), decision junction (page 4), glossary and grievance phone tree (page 37).
